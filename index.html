Eating Disorders

9% of Americans will have an eating disorder in their lifetime. It is important to be able to recognize the signs and symptoms, so you know when a friend or a loved one needs to get help.

Selective Eating Disorder

Selective Eating Disorder, also known as Avoidant or Restrictive Food Intake Disorder (ARFID), can also be known as picky eating, but to the extreme. It mainly impacts children.

Night Eating Syndrome

Night Eating Syndrome can often be confused with Binge Eating Disorder, but they are very different. With Night Eating Syndrome, there is a problem with the circadian pattern of food intake. Although some people with Night Eating Syndrome may binge, they more so eat more at night than during the day.

Binge Eating Disorder

47% of people with eating disorders have Binge Eating Disorder. People with Binge Eating Disorder experience a lack of control, and eating excessive amounts of food in short amounts of time. Binge Eating Disorder can happen to anyone, of any age group, geographic locations, or gender.

Pica

Pica is an eating disorder in which a person eatings things that are not food, and provide no nutritional value.
